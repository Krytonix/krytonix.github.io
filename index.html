About Krytonix Seed Finder

Krytonix Seed Finder lets you paste any Minecraft seed and instantly open a world map (via Chunkbase). Choose edition & version, explore villages, strongholds, slime chunks and teleport coordinates.

How to use

  1. Click Open Map (or the image button).
  2. Enter/paste your Minecraft seed in the prompt and press OK.
  3. Chunkbase will open in a new tab with the seed — explore structures there.

Tips

  • Use Java edition seeds for Java worlds; pick version carefully (1.8/1.16/1.20...)
  • Upload level.dat in future to auto-read seed (coming soon).
  • Mobile: rotate to landscape for wider map view.
