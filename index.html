About Krytonix Seed Finder

Krytonix Seed Finder lets you paste any Minecraft seed and instantly open a world map using Chunkbase. Explore biomes, structures, villages, and more — perfect for Java & Bedrock players.

How to Use

  1. Click Open Map button above.
  2. Enter or paste your Minecraft seed.
  3. Chunkbase will open and generate your world map.

Tips

  • Use correct edition (Java / Bedrock) for accurate results.
  • Choose your Minecraft version carefully (1.8–1.20+ supported).
  • Mobile users: rotate screen for full map view.
