About Krytonix Seed Finder

Krytonix Seed Finder lets you paste any Minecraft seed and instantly view its world map using Chunkbase.

How to Use

  1. Click Open Map on the homepage.
  2. Enter or paste your Minecraft seed.
  3. Chunkbase will generate the map automatically.

Tips

  • Use correct edition (Java / Bedrock) for accuracy.
  • Select correct Minecraft version (1.8–1.20+ supported).
  • Rotate phone to landscape for better map view.
